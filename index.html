One-to-One Training

We are a charity teaching computer skills offering one-to-one computer training to the over 50s. Our sessions are on Tuesdays at 10.30 am to 12.30 pm and 1.00 pm to 3 pm, and on Wednesdays at 10.30 am to 12.30 pm in central Edinburgh.

Questions about your iPad or Kindle? Problem with your phone? Many thousands of older people have solved their technology issues at one of our sessions.

  • Our sessions are on:
  • Tuesdays:
  • 10.30am-12.30pm and
  • 1.00pm-3pm
  • Wednesdays:
  • 10.30am-12.30pm

Bring in your own device – laptop, tablet, smartphone, camera – and one of our experts will help you with it. Our friendly volunteers make learning about technology easy. Find out about the internet, email, Skype, Windows, managing photos and attachments … whatever you want!

Call us to book your session, or for more information. We’d love to hear from you.
